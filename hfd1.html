Description

EverHerb Diabetic Care Juice has been formulated with 11 handpicked herbs chosen for the high regard Ayurveda has for them. Let the goodness of Jamun, Amla, Karela, Guduchi, Tulsi, Neem, Belpatra, Methi, Kutki, Gudmaar, and Vijaysar help you be in charge of your blood sugar level and overall health.

High blood sugar levels can lead to many complications associated with nerves, kidneys, and eyes, over a period of time. There are many ways to manage blood sugar level such as lifestyle modifications, regular exercise, and altering food habits. The kind of food you ingest also plays an important role in the maintenance of normal blood glucose levels.

The 11 herbs present in EverHerb Diabetic Care Juice can help with the symptoms of high blood sugar such as the need to frequently urinate, bodyweight fluctuation, excessive appetite, and thirst. EverHerb Diabetic Care Juice can aid in restoring glucose balance, and also helps with sugar metabolism. These herbs are sourced from the best farms to ensure that only the most potent ingredients are used in the making of this health supplement.

EverHerb Diabetic Care Juice does not have any added sugars. The formula is completely vegan and hence suited for the consumption of everyone.

Key Benefits:

  1. Helps with blood sugar break-down

Dilute 20-30 ml of EverHerb Diabetic Care Juice in a glass of water and consume it 2 times a day on an empty stomach, once in the morning and again in the evening. Shake the bottle well before use.
